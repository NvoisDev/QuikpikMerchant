How to access:
  1. Log into your Quikpik dashboard
  2. Click on "Customer Groups" in the sidebar
  3. Look for the "Merge Duplicates" button in the top toolbar

The Customer Groups page contains all customer management tools, including the merge functionality.

üîÑ Step 2: Initiate Customer Merge

Two ways to merge:
  1. Automatic Detection: Click "Merge Duplicates" button to auto-detect duplicate customers with the same phone number
  2. Manual Selection: Select customers individually and choose merge option

The system will automatically find customers with matching phone numbers and similar names.
